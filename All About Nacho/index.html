About Me

Hi, I’m Nacho, I’m on a mission to explore, learn, and grow every single day. I have a HUGE passion for knowledge and a deep love for figuring out how things work. Whether it’s diving into a challenging subject at school or exploring my hobbies, I’m always ready to take on something new. I work hard and feel proud to earn A's and B's, knowing that effort and curiosity are the keys to success.

My faith is a big part of who I am. I love God and feel inspired every day to live with kindness, purpose, and a positive attitude. I believe that with God’s help, there’s no limit to what I can achieve!

I’m also blessed to have an amazing family that supports and loves me. I’m really close with my two brothers, my sister, and my mom. They mean the world to me, and I’m thankful for all the fun times we have together and the way they encourage me to always do my best.

Outside of school, I have so many interests that keep me busy and excited. Basketball is one of my favorite activities—there’s nothing like hitting the court, improving my skills, and feeling the excitement of the game. I’m also a huge fan of gaming. Whether it’s exploring the worlds of horror games, solving puzzles, or teaming up with friends in online adventures, I always have a blast. I’m especially drawn to anything that involves terminals or command prompts. Coding and experimenting with technology make me feel like a real-life problem-solver, and I love watching ideas come to life on a screen.

I believe my passion for learning, my love for family, and my ability to balance hard work with fun will take me far. Whether I’m coding, playing basketball, gaming, or learning something new, I give it my all. Life is an adventure, and I’m excited to see where my curiosity, creativity, and faith will take me next.
